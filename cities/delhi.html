New Delhi

Welcome to New Delhi!

New Delhi, the capital city of India, is a vibrant metropolis with a rich history.

India Gate

A war memorial and a popular tourist attraction.

Red Fort

A UNESCO World Heritage site known for its stunning Mughal architecture.

Qutub Minar

The tallest brick minaret in the world, surrounded by beautiful ruins.

Lotus Temple

A Bahá'í House of Worship famous for its lotus-like architecture.

Lodi Gardens

A tranquil park dotted with historical tombs and monuments.

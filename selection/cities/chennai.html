Welcome to Chennai!

Chennai, the capital of Tamil Nadu, is known for its cultural heritage and beautiful beaches.

Mahabalipuram

A UNESCO World Heritage site famous for its rock-cut temples.

Marina Beach

One of the longest beaches in the world, perfect for evening strolls.

Kapaleeshwarar Temple

A beautiful temple known for its intricate architecture.

Vandalur Zoo

The first zoological park in India, home to diverse wildlife.

Chennai Museum

One of the largest museums in India with an extensive collection.
